# The Saviour — Art Direction

Status: gameplay-first direction approved 2026-07-20.

## Visual identity

The game combines a readable stylized 3D gothic diorama with painterly title and
bookend art. Gameplay must remain visually dominant: silhouettes, telegraphs,
enemy state, portals, pickups, and combat effects take priority over decoration.

## Gameplay layer

- Zephyr reads as a fast reaper with a long, clean scythe silhouette.
- Stable and volatile enemy variants are distinguished through form, color,
  motion, and effects without explanatory labels.
- Biomes vary through architecture, props, palette, fog, and lighting while
  preserving collision and telegraph contrast.
- Reward presentation uses the project-original scythe-dial framework with live
  text and separately interactive Reaper, Shade, and Grave stations.
- HUD ornament may be gothic, but health, Harvest, dash energy, prompts, and the
  final timer remain immediately legible.

## VN layer

The full-screen illustrated layer is reserved for the standard opening and both
endings. It uses the canonical Zephyr C and Elowen A designs, restrained camera
motion, one readable text box, and one Continue action. Do not produce floor,
reward, or companion-scene variants.

## Asset rules

- Runtime character and background files are listed in `docs/ASSET_MATRIX.md`.
- New external assets require verified rights and an update to
  `public/assets/LICENSES.md`.
- Do not reintroduce retired VN images simply because they remain in old commits
  or screenshots.
