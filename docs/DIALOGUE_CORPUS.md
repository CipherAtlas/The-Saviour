## Word-count contract

Displayed dialogue is counted from the final column of each beat table. IDs,
metadata, headings, controls, upgrade-card text, and glossary copy are excluded.
The unique corpus and both playable routes must each remain between 4,000 and
6,000 words. `ending.decision` contains no dialogue and is therefore absent from
the 49-sequence registry.
