# Music Research and Licensing Record

Research completed: 2026-07-18  
Scope: the eight cues packaged in `public/assets/audio/music/`, plus relevant candidates rejected during the cohesive-cue search.

## Selection decision

The accepted soundtrack is an instrumental gothic-chamber/orchestral family by Kevin MacLeod. It uses three related ideas rather than a collection of unrelated dramatic tracks:

1. **Paired-ring devotion:** exposed harp in “Lamentation,” then harp and celesta in both “Darkest Child” arrangements.
2. **The Witch's ordered containment:** the three “Constancy” movements share strings, low woodwinds, bells, and a controlled escalation across boss phases.
3. **Elowen's corruption:** the childlike “Darkest Child” material accelerates for combat, then gives way to the unstable full-orchestra language of “Unlight.”

The runtime deliberately reuses those families. “Darkest Child” carries domestic opening, absence and pursuit, exploration, Witch projections, upgrade intimacy, the unstable aftermath, Elowen's reveal, and the five-second decision; dialogue ducking preserves text clarity. “Darkest Child var A” carries ordinary combat. A per-biome low-pass profile changes the licensed exploration/combat timbre across the Forgotten Keep, Ossuary, Ember Foundry, and Void Court without introducing unrelated music. “Lamentation” carries the title and post-run return, the “Constancy” triptych carries the Witch battle, and the two non-looping ending cues separate mercy from release.

## Rights and acquisition terms shared by every accepted cue

Each accepted track's official Incompetech page identifies Kevin MacLeod as creator, exposes a direct MP3 download, prints the track-specific attribution, and applies **Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International**. The [authoritative CC BY 4.0 deed](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/) permits sharing and adaptation in any medium for any purpose, including commercial use, provided appropriate credit and a license link are supplied and modifications are indicated. It also prohibits downstream legal or technological restrictions that would prevent recipients from exercising the licensed rights. The [legal code](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/legalcode.en) expressly permits reproduction, redistribution, adapted material, all media/formats, and necessary technical modifications.

- **Price / acquisition:** no-cost individual MP3 download from each official source page; no purchase, subscription, account, or repost mirror was used. The catalog's optional paid bundles and store links were not needed.
- **Commercial use:** permitted under CC BY 4.0.
- **Modification:** permitted. The packaged copies were technically transcoded to 128 kbps, 44.1 kHz stereo and stripped of source metadata for web delivery; no musical content was added.
- **Redistribution:** permitted under CC BY 4.0 with attribution, license notice/link, and modification notice. No additional restriction may be imposed on the licensed material.
- **Source format / quality:** the individual official pages advertise MP3 downloads. They do not publish the source MP3 bitrate. The release files' properties below were independently measured with `ffprobe`.
- **Stems:** no stems are advertised on any of the eight individual source pages. The paired “Darkest Child” arrangements and three separately mastered “Constancy” parts are complete alternate cues, not stems.
- **Key policy:** no named musical key is published on the individual pages. This record therefore says “not published; not independently measured” rather than guessing. “Constancy Part One” is officially described as minor, but its tonic is not identified.

## Compliance conclusion

- No ambiguous license, unverified download mirror, paid acquisition, subscription, or account-gated source was accepted.
- No voice acting, sung part, choir, vocal effect, breath, effort, or combat bark was accepted into the packaged music set.
- The exact runtime attribution is present in the in-game Credits and `public/assets/LICENSES.md`; the required modification notice is also recorded there.
- The accepted eight-cue set is source-backed, commercially usable under CC BY 4.0, internally cohesive, and mapped to the runtime files and cue keys without inventing keys, stems, source quality, or loop points.
